## Reasoning
The selection of **STUB** as the winning stock is based on the "Insider Confidence Reversal" strategy. The market reacted violently to StubHub's negative free cash flow report, pushing the stock down over 20%. This created a deep value pocket. The immediate entry of RPM Capital with a substantial $7.25 million position validates the company's long-term value at these levels and likely marks a bottom.

In the next 10 days, we anticipate a continuation of the relief rally started on Jan 15. The 11-16% bounce seen recently is likely just the start of a mean reversion trade. The "fear" from the lawsuits is priced in, while the "greed" from the insider buy is just starting to price in. We expect STUB to attempt to fill the gap created by the earnings drop, offering a high probability of >10% returns from the $15.20 level (targeting ~$17+). FIGR is a safer long-term hold, but STUB offers the aggressive 10-day return potential we are seeking.
