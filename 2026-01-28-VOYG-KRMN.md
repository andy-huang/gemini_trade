# VOYG and Winning Stock

## Conclusion
The original stock, **Voyager Technologies (VOYG)**, shows promise with a recent NASA partnership and institutional buying, but remains a mixed bag due to recent price volatility. The "Winning Stock" identified in this group is **Karman Holdings Inc. (KRMN)**. Karman distinguishes itself from the broader space/defense peer group by delivering not just revenue growth, but explosive **net income growth (+78.5%)**. While peers like Firefly and AIRO struggle with profitability, Karman is executing a profitable growth strategy fueled by strategic acquisitions and expanding margins, driving a ~40% year-to-date stock rally.

## Reasoning
**Karman Holdings (KRMN)** is the winner because **profitability matters**. In a high-growth sector like space/defense, companies that can grow the bottom line (Net Income) alongside the top line are rare and command a premium.
1.  **Fundamental Superiority:** +78% net income growth is a standout metric that validates their operational efficiency and M&A integration strategy.
2.  **Defensive Moat:** Expansion into marine defense diversifies them beyond just "space," providing a more stable government revenue base.
3.  **Price Action:** The stock's steady 40% rise YTD confirms that institutional investors are rewarding this profitable growth profile over speculative peers.
