# VG and Winning Stock

## Conclusion
The original stock was **Venture Global LNG (VG)**, which has seen positive momentum (+7.3%) driven by strong export volumes and a new long-term sales agreement with Greece. However, the "Winning Stock" identified is **EQT Corporation (EQT)**. EQT carries a "Strong Buy" analyst consensus and is projected to grow its fiscal 2025 profits by nearly 80%. As the largest natural gas producer in the U.S., EQT is best positioned to capitalize on the recovering gas market dynamics that are also lifting VG.
