# VOYG and Winning Stock

## Conclusion
The original stock **VOYG (Voyager Technologies)** is fundamentally sound with recent NASA agreements, but the stock is currently underperforming (-6% recently) due to lack of immediate explosive catalysts compared to its peers.

The **Winning Stock** is **RDW (Redwire Corporation)**. RDW has surged ~28% today after being selected for the massive $151 billion U.S. Department of Defense SHIELD contract. This transformational news has made it the clear leader in the space/defense sector today, significantly outperforming peers like VOYG and CHYM.

VOYG, while solid, cannot compete with this level of immediate catalyst.
