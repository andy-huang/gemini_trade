## Analysis Summary
The analysis focused on **FIGR (Figure Technology Solutions, Inc.)** and its "People Also Watch" peers: **GEMI, BLSH, KLAR, CHYM, and STUB**.

## Winning Stock Selection
**Winning Stock: FIGR**

### Reasoning
FIGR remains the clear standout for a >10% potential return in the short term:
1.  **Analyst Conviction:** The Bernstein "Top Pick" designation is a powerful driver that is likely to attract continued institutional flow.
2.  **Valuation Gap:** With a target of $72 and current price ~$53, there is a substantial ~35% upside gap that the market is likely to close.
3.  **Unique Catalyst:** The "tokenized equity" narrative is unique to FIGR and positions it as a leader in a new financial vertical, separating it from generic fintechs like Chime or Klarna.
